# Requirements: Rule 257 NYC

**Defined:** 2026-04-09
**Core Value:** Authentically represent Rule 257's identity as a space where art, fashion, design, and coffee culture converge

## v1 Requirements

Requirements for initial release. Each maps to roadmap phases.

### Brand Site

- [ ] **BRAND-01**: Visitor sees full-bleed hero section with strong visual identity, sharp typography, and minimal brand intro
- [ ] **BRAND-02**: Visitor can find location (54 Eldridge St), hours, phone (+1 917-774-7263), and contact info with Google Maps embed
- [ ] **BRAND-03**: Site includes Schema.org LocalBusiness markup for Google Knowledge Panel
- [ ] **BRAND-04**: Visitor can browse signature menu items (French vanilla oat latte, strawberry matcha latte, pistachio tiramisu) with styled product cards
- [ ] **BRAND-05**: Visitor can browse photo gallery of the space in masonry/grid layout with lazy-loaded optimized images
- [ ] **BRAND-06**: Site is fully mobile-responsive with mobile-first design
- [ ] **BRAND-07**: Social media links visible in footer (Instagram, etc.)

### Content

- [ ] **CONT-01**: Visitor can browse upcoming and past events with date, title, description, and image
- [ ] **CONT-02**: Visitor can view artist collaboration profiles with name, bio, portfolio images, and external links
- [ ] **CONT-03**: Visitor can browse editorial lookbook pages with magazine-style fashion/art/design layouts
- [ ] **CONT-04**: Visitor can see curated Instagram content from @rule257.nyc on the site

### Loyalty

- [ ] **LOYAL-01**: Customer can create an account with email and password
- [ ] **LOYAL-02**: Customer can log in and stay logged in across sessions
- [ ] **LOYAL-03**: Customer can view their points balance and transaction history
- [ ] **LOYAL-04**: Customer can display a personal QR code for staff to scan at the register
- [ ] **LOYAL-05**: Staff can scan customer QR codes to award points for purchases
- [ ] **LOYAL-06**: Customer can browse a rewards catalog with point costs
- [ ] **LOYAL-07**: Customer can redeem points for rewards with staff confirmation
- [ ] **LOYAL-08**: Staff can access an admin panel to manage points, view customers, and confirm redemptions
- [ ] **LOYAL-09**: QR codes use server-generated tokens with expiry to prevent fraud

### Mobile

- [ ] **MOBILE-01**: iOS app via Capacitor with native feel (haptics, camera access for QR)
- [ ] **MOBILE-02**: Android app via Capacitor with native feel
- [ ] **MOBILE-03**: Mobile apps lead with loyalty features (not just wrapped website) for App Store compliance

### Polish

- [ ] **POLISH-01**: Dark mode / light mode toggle with system preference detection
- [ ] **POLISH-02**: Scroll-driven animations and micro-interactions (parallax, entrance reveals)
- [ ] **POLISH-03**: Curated Spotify/Apple Music playlist links for cafe atmosphere

## Out of Scope

Explicitly excluded. Documented to prevent scope creep.

| Feature | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Full online ordering system | Brand-first, not a commerce platform. Drive foot traffic |
| Reservations / booking | Walk-in cafe, booking implies scarcity that doesn't exist |
| Blog / CMS | Editorial managed through codebase per project decision |
| Real-time chat / support | Instagram DMs and phone already serve this purpose |
| User-generated content / reviews | 4.9/5 on Google with 170 reviews already. Instagram surfaces authentic customer photos |
| Complex gamification (badges, streaks) | Over-engineering for single-location. Simple points work better |
| Multi-language support | Brand voice is English. Visual-heavy design transcends language |
| Multi-location support | Single location at 54 Eldridge St. Don't build for hypothetical expansion |
